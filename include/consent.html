Thank you for joining the Typo Challenge!

You are being invited to participate in a research study entitled the “Typo challenge”. This study is being led by Dr Anne Cori from Imperial College London and Dr Marc Baguelin from Public Health England.

The purpose of this research study is to understand the errors made when people enter dates in health records, so that these errors can be automatically identified and corrected in epidemic data. You can read more about the study here ).

Your participation in this study is entirely voluntary and you can withdraw at any time. You are free to omit to answer any question.

At the end of the study, the data from the Typo Challenge will be analysed and then the results will be summarised in a scientific publication, as well as made publically available with the publication.

We are not collecting any data that would allow you to be identified, and we believe there are no known risks associated with this research study.

You must be 18 years old or over to take part in this study.
